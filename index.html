Dine-in Only Processor — use this when you don't have an Online Menu file at all, just the Offline (Dine-in) Menu. It adds an Area Display Name column right after Online Display Name (left blank), and automatically marks known non-food items (tissues, cutlery, packaging charges, alcohol, decoration/event charges, etc.) as Available = No. Everything else keeps its original Available value. This works completely independently of the merge tool above.
